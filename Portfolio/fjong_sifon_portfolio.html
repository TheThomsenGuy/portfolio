  1. Case: Create a Magazine for fellow students
  2. Occation: 1. semster project
  3. Duration: 14 days
  4. Team: Myself
  5. Goal: Create content and value for targeted audience
  6. Keypoints: Interviews, Datamining, Content production, IA, Usability,
  7. Personas, Photography, Videography.

The project

This was my first solo project. I started off with planning my schedule
based on requirements and necessary steps to create a magazine
that fit my fellow students. The main task was to write articles and the
information architecture, usability testing and personas were
crucial to the product. I think that the time limitation and my skills
at this point of my study shows in the design, nevertheless i can
safely say that I learned from the process and had great results
with UX development for the targeted user.
